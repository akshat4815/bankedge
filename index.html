The PROBE (Professional Banking Executive) program is a 150-hour certification course tailored for aspiring retail bankers. It provides a deep dive into banking products, processes, and operations, equipping you with the skills necessary for entry-level positions in retail banking.

The PROBE program is structured to be completed in 3 months through full-time weekday sessions. For those with different scheduling needs, we offer a 2-month hybrid format that combines online and offline learning. For more details on schedules, please contact our nearest center.

Our program includes 150 hours of in-person classroom instruction along with access to our advanced Learning Management System (LMS). You will receive PowerPoint presentations, case studies, quizzes, and eBooks through the LMS. Your provided credentials will give you access to these valuable resources.

Upon completion of the PROBE program, you will be prepared for various entry-level roles in retail banking such as Personal Banker, Relationship Manager, Customer Service Executive, and Loan Officer. Our program is designed to help you excel in these positions and advance your career.

Retail banking, or consumer banking, involves providing financial services directly to individuals rather than businesses. Services include savings and transaction accounts, mortgages, personal loans, and credit cards. It is different from investment and commercial banking, focusing on the everyday financial needs of individual customers.

The PROBE program offers a unique blend of practical training and industry insights that are not typically available in traditional banking and finance degrees.
