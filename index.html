Curriculum Insight:

Introduction to Banking

Gain a foundational understanding of banking principles, industry structures, and key operational processes essential for a successful career in finance.

Trade Finance Operations

Explore the complexities of international trade finance, including letter of credit management, trade risk mitigation, and global payment systems.

Retail Banking Operations

Delve into the core functions of retail banking, focusing on customer service, account management, and everyday banking transactions.

Credit Administration

Learn the intricacies of credit risk assessment, loan processing, and credit portfolio management to ensure sound lending practices.

Cash Management Service

Master the techniques for efficient cash flow management, including liquidity solutions, cash forecasting, and electronic payment systems.

Security Information System

Understand the essential frameworks for protecting financial data, managing cybersecurity risks, and implementing robust security protocols.

Financial Crime Management

Examine strategies for combating financial crimes, including fraud detection, anti-money laundering practices, and regulatory compliance.

Interview Preparation & Soft Skills

Enhance your employability with tailored interview preparation techniques and the development of essential soft skills for professional success.
